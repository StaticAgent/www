Hello!

I’m F. Emerson. In the Internet, I also go by the handle StaticAgent. I’m a software engineer and aspiring game programmer; I have been developing software professionaly since 2015, focusing mostly on systems programming, server-side web development, database design and game server programming.

Nowadays, I work at Aquiris, a brazilian game studio based in Porto Alegre, as a back-end engineer. I’m currently part of the team working on Looney Tunes™: World of Mayhem.

Regarding my academic background, I hold a B.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the Federal University of Paraíba (UFPB). My research interests are: high performance, photorealistic rendering; parallel and distributed computing; and stochastic optimization.

In my spare time, I like to ramble about how the so-called modern software is synonymous with low-quality, resource-wasteful software. I also spend time working on side projects and reading stuff. Sometimes, I write about my thoughts and experiences on these things.
